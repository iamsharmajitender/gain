## Key Practices

<Details className="gain-accordion" summary="Start with evaluation criteria">
  Define success metrics before building. Precision, recall, latency, and cost targets should drive every architectural decision: not the other way around.
</Details>

<Details className="gain-accordion" summary="Instrument from day one">
  Log every retrieval, generation, and user interaction from the first deployment. You cannot improve what you cannot measure: and RAG systems degrade silently without observability.
</Details>

<Details className="gain-accordion" summary="Iterate on retrieval first">
  Most RAG quality problems are retrieval problems, not generation problems. Invest in chunking, indexing, and reranking before switching models or rewriting prompts.
</Details>

<Details className="gain-accordion" summary="Plan for data drift">
  Source documents change, embeddings go stale, and user queries evolve. Build re-indexing pipelines and freshness checks into the operating model from the start.
</Details>
