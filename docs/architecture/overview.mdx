## What lives here

- **Distributed systems**: resilience, scale, and observability
- **Event-driven systems**: loose coupling, streams, and async workflows
- **API design**: contracts, versioning, and integration boundaries
- **Governance & security**: policy, compliance, and operational trust at platform level
